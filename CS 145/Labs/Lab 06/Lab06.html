Lab 06

What is your Zodiac?

 

Objective:

 

Write a program that determines a person’s astrological zodiac sign based on their birthday.

 

  • Prompt the user for a month and then a day
  • The month and day have to be valid
    • Month: 1-12
    • Day: 1-31
    • You do not have to check if it the date is real.  Such as April 31st is not a real date.  That’s a whole different problem.
  • Then determine the person’s zodiac symbol
    • Aries = March 21 to April 19
    • Taurus = April 20 to May 20
    • Gemini = May 21 to June 20
    • Cancer = June 21 to July 22
    • Leo = July 23 to August 22
    • Virgo = August 23 to September 22
    • Libra = September 23 to October 22
    • Scorpio = October 23 to November 21
    • Sagittarius = November 22 to December 21
    • Capricorn = December 22 to January 19
    • Aquarius = January 20 to February 18
    • Pisces = February 19 to March 20
  • If the data was incorrect (like April 31st) then you do not have to print out anything.

 

 

Example Dialog:

 

What is your zodiac?  Enter your birthday! Month followed by Day!

6 22

You are a cancer!

 

Another Example Dialog:

What is your zodiac?  Enter your birthday! Month followed by Day!

-6 22

Invalid month or day!
